Things I never
said out loud

To you, on your birthday —

There are things I've wanted to say for a while now, and I've found every excuse not to. But it feels wrong to let today pass without being honest — even just a little.

I notice you. More than I probably should. The way you carry yourself in a room, the way you talk about things you care about — you do it with this kind of quiet confidence that I've never quite seen in anyone else. And it gets to me, every single time.

I don't know exactly what this is. I'm not here to make things complicated or put anything on you that you didn't ask for. I just — I'd feel dishonest celebrating you today without telling you that being around you genuinely makes things feel lighter.

You've made me want to be more thoughtful. More present. More like someone worth knowing — and I don't think you realize how much that matters, or how rare it is for one person to do that to another.

So this is me saying it, on the one day that feels right to say it: I like you. Not in a way that asks anything of you. Just — in the realest, most genuine way I know. And I'm really glad you exist.

— yours, somehow. ♡
